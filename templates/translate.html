About our translator

For those whose first language is not Telugu, typing and translating English to Telugu can be difficult. Those services can either be expensive or if it is free the quality of translation is often poor and unreliable. While it makes sense for paying professionals to translate highly specialized subjects and official documents - there is no need to pay for translating commonly spoken words and phrases.
